# Atlas Airways Ticket Types and Change/Cancellation Rules

## Ticket Types Overview

### Atlas Go (Economy)

- Most restrictive fare type.
- Changes and cancellations incur fees.
- All changes must be made before the scheduled departure time.

### Atlas Plus (Premium Economy)

- More flexible than Go fares.
- **Plus Pro**: Allows free changes and cancellations.
- **Plus Smart** (non-Pro): Lower change/cancellation fees compared to Go.

### Atlas Business

- Most flexible fare type.
- **Business Pro**: Allows unlimited free rebooking. Cancellations with full refunds.
- **Business Smart**: Standard fees apply but lower than economy fares.

## Rebooking Fees

| Route Type | Approximate Fee |
|---|---|
| European routes | ~€70–€80 |
| Intercontinental routes | ~$300–$450 |
| Plus Pro / Business Pro | Free (pay only fare difference) |

## Name Changes

- Name changes are possible before the ticket is used.
- Fee: approximately **600 DKK / NOK / SEK**.

## 24-Hour Money-Back Guarantee

- You can cancel any booking for free within **24 hours** of purchase, regardless of fare type.
- Full refund to the original payment method.

## Award / Bonus Tickets

- Rebooking or cancellation fees for award tickets:
  - **Short-haul**: €25
  - **Long-haul**: €75

## If Atlas Airways Cancels Your Flight

- You are entitled to a **full refund** or **free rebooking** to an alternative flight, regardless of your fare type.

## Third-Party Bookings

- If you booked through a travel agent or third-party website, contact them directly for changes or refunds.
- Atlas Airways cannot modify bookings made through external channels.
