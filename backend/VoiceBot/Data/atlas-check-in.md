# Atlas Airways Check-In and Boarding

## Online Check-In

- Available **30 hours to 1 hour** before departure.
- Check in via the Atlas Airways website or the Atlas Airways app.
- Receive a digital boarding pass on your phone or print at home.
- If carry-on only with a digital boarding pass, proceed straight to security.

### Not Available For

- Passengers requiring special assistance
- Unaccompanied minors
- Passengers traveling with pets
- Passengers carrying firearms
- Passengers with musical instruments requiring special handling

## Airport Check-In Deadlines

| Region | Check-In / Bag Drop Closes | Boarding Closes |
|---|---|---|
| Scandinavia & Europe | 45 min before departure | 20 min before departure |
| Greenland | 60 min before departure | 20 min before departure |
| Asia & North America (Economy/Premium) | 90 min before departure | 35 min before departure |
| Asia & North America (Business) | 60 min before departure | 35 min before departure |
| Africa | 60 min before departure | 20 min before departure |
| Charter flights | 60 min before departure | 20 min before departure |

## Counter Opening Times

- **European/Scandinavian flights**: Counters open 2–4 hours before departure.
- **Intercontinental flights**: Counters open 3–4 hours before departure.

## Self-Service Kiosks

- Available at most airports served by Atlas Airways.
- Use kiosks to check in and print bag tags.

## Recommended Arrival Times

| Flight Type | Arrive at Airport |
|---|---|
| European / Scandinavian | At least 2 hours before departure |
| Intercontinental | At least 3 hours before departure |
