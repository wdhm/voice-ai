# Atlas Airways Special Assistance

## Wheelchair and Mobility Assistance

- Request at least **48 hours before your flight** via your booking, "My Bookings", the Atlas Airways app, or Customer Service.
- Assistance is available from check-in through to the aircraft and upon arrival.
- If you are unable to handle safety-related tasks independently (such as fastening a seatbelt or putting on an oxygen mask), a personal assistant must accompany you.
- Atlas Airways complies with **EU Regulation 1107/2006** regarding the rights of disabled persons and persons with reduced mobility.

## Unaccompanied Minors (UM)

- **Mandatory** for children aged **5–11** traveling without an adult.
- **Optional** for children aged **12–17**.
- Book online for European flights, or via Atlas Airways Customer Service for intercontinental flights.
- Children are escorted from check-in to the designated pickup person at the destination.
- The **UM form** must be filled out and printed before travel.

## Traveling with Children

- Child ticket discounts are available on most routes.
- Special baggage allowances apply for families traveling with children.
- Child meals can be requested in advance.
- **Car seats** can be used on board (must be aviation-approved).
- **Bassinets** are available on long-haul flights for infants — request in advance.

## Pets

- **Service dogs** are welcome in the cabin. Notify Atlas Airways at least 48 hours before departure and complete the required forms.
- **Pet travel** (cabin or checked baggage) depends on the pet's size, route, and destination regulations.
- Book early — availability for pets is limited per flight.

## Medical Needs

- Notify Atlas Airways at the time of booking if you require medical equipment or special support.
- A **doctor's note** or **Atlas Airways medical clearance** may be required for certain medical conditions.
- Assistance is available for passengers with cognitive, visual, or hearing impairments.

## How to Request Assistance

- Via the **"My Bookings"** portal on the Atlas Airways website or app.
- Via **Atlas Airways Customer Service** contact forms.
- All requests should be made as early as possible, ideally at the time of booking.
