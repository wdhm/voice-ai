# Atlas Airways SkyPoints Loyalty Program

## Membership Tiers

Atlas Airways SkyPoints has four membership levels:

| Tier | Qualification (Rolling 12 Months) |
|---|---|
| Member | Automatic upon enrollment |
| Silver | 20,000 level points or 10 qualifying flights |
| Gold | 45,000 level points or 45 qualifying flights |
| Diamond | 90,000 level points or 90 qualifying flights |

## Point Types

- **Level points**: Used for status qualification. Earned on flights and determine your membership tier.
- **Bonus points**: Used for rewards. Can be redeemed for flights, upgrades, and more.

## Earning Points

- Points are earned based on flight class, booking code, and distance flown.
- Additional earning opportunities: hotels, car rentals, credit card spending, and partner purchases.

### Point Multipliers by Tier

| Tier | Bonus Multiplier |
|---|---|
| Silver | +25% |
| Gold | +50% |
| Diamond | +75% |

## Redeeming Points

- Award flights (Atlas Airways and Global Alliance partners)
- Cabin upgrades
- Hotel bookings
- Car rentals
- Merchandise and experiences

## Benefits by Tier

### Silver Benefits

- 1 extra checked bag
- Priority check-in and boarding
- 5% discount on award bookings

### Gold Benefits

- All Silver benefits, plus:
- 2 extra checked bags
- Fast Track security access
- Atlas Airways and Global Alliance lounge access
- Free Wi-Fi on Atlas Airways flights
- Priority baggage handling
- Guaranteed seat on full flights (booked 24+ hours in advance)

### Diamond Benefits

- All Gold benefits, plus:
- No point expiry (expiry suspended while Diamond status is held)
- Free drinks in economy class
- Free same-day rebooking
- No award booking fees

## Alliance

- Atlas Airways is a **Global Alliance member** (previously part of Star Alliance).
- SkyPoints can be earned and redeemed on Global Alliance partner airlines.

## Point Expiry

- Points expire after **4–5 years** unless activity is recorded in the account.
- Diamond members: Point expiry is suspended for the duration of Diamond status.
