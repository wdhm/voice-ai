# Atlas Airways Cabin Baggage Rules

## Carry-On Bag (Overhead Bin)

- **Maximum dimensions**: 55 × 40 × 23 cm
- **Maximum weight**: 8 kg
- Stored in the overhead compartment

## Personal Item (Under Seat)

- **Maximum dimensions**: 40 × 30 × 15 cm
- Stored under the seat in front of you
- Examples: handbag, laptop bag, small backpack

## Allowance by Ticket Type

| Ticket Type | Carry-On (Overhead) | Personal Item (Under Seat) |
|---|---|---|
| Economy Light (Europe) | Not included (extra cost) | 1 included |
| Economy Light (US/Canada/Asia) | 1 included | 1 included |
| Go Smart / Go Pro | 1 included | 1 included |
| Atlas Plus (short-haul) | 1 included | 1 included |
| Atlas Plus (long-haul) | 2 included | 1 included |
| Business | 2 included (each max 8 kg) | 1 included |

## Economy Light — Purchasing Overhead Carry-On

- Economy Light tickets on European routes only include a personal item.
- An overhead carry-on bag can be purchased as an add-on.
- Purchasing in advance online is cheaper than buying at the airport.

## Size and Weight Enforcement

- Atlas Airways performs strict size and weight checks at the boarding gate.
- Bags exceeding the limits may be checked to the hold for an additional fee.

## SkyPoints Members

- SkyPoints Silver members and above may receive extra cabin baggage allowances, even on Light fares.

## Liquids in Cabin Baggage

- All liquid containers must be **maximum 100 ml** each.
- All containers must fit in a **single transparent, resealable bag** (max 1 liter capacity).
- The bag must be presented separately at security screening.
