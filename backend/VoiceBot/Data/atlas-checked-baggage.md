# Atlas Airways Checked Baggage

## Free Checked Baggage by Ticket Type

| Ticket Type | Free Checked Bags | Weight per Bag |
|---|---|---|
| Atlas Go Light | None (must pay extra) | — |
| Atlas Go Smart / Go Pro | 1 bag | Up to 23 kg |
| Atlas Plus Smart / Plus Pro | 2 bags | Up to 23 kg each |
| Atlas Business | 2 bags | Up to 32 kg each |

## Maximum Dimensions

- **158 cm total** (length + width + height) per checked bag.

## Extra Checked Baggage Fees

- Fees range from **€20 to €105** per extra bag, each way, depending on route and destination.
- Booking extra baggage online in advance is always cheaper than purchasing at the airport.

## Overweight Baggage

- Bags weighing **23.1–32 kg**: Overweight fee of **€55–€135** on European routes.
- Bags weighing **over 32 kg**: Not accepted as checked luggage. Must be shipped as cargo.

## Children's Equipment

- **Strollers and booster seats** are carried free of charge for children up to 11 years old.
- These do not count toward the baggage allowance.

## SkyPoints and Global Alliance Elite Members

- **SkyPoints Silver** and **Global Alliance Elite** members receive one extra free checked bag on most routes.
- This is in addition to the baggage allowance included with the ticket type.
