# Atlas Airways Delays and Cancellations — Passenger Rights

## EU261 Regulation Coverage

EU Regulation 261/2004 applies to:

- All flights departing from any EU airport.
- All flights arriving in the EU operated by Atlas Airways.
- Also covers Iceland, Norway, and Switzerland.

## Compensation Amounts

| Flight Distance | Compensation |
|---|---|
| Up to 1,500 km | €250 |
| Intra-EU over 1,500 km, or other flights 1,500–3,500 km | €400 |
| Flights over 3,500 km outside EU | €600 |

## Eligibility

- **Delays**: Arrival at final destination delayed by **3 or more hours**.
- **Cancellations**: Flight cancelled within **14 days** of the scheduled departure date.

## Extraordinary Circumstances

Compensation may not apply if the disruption was caused by extraordinary circumstances, including:

- Severe weather conditions
- Security threats
- Air traffic control strikes
- Political instability

Technical and operational issues are considered the airline's responsibility and do not exempt Atlas Airways from compensation.

## Your Rights During Disruptions

### Rebooking

- Alternative flight as soon as possible, or rebooking to a later date of your choice.
- Or a **full ticket refund** if you choose not to travel.

### Care and Assistance

- Meals and refreshments proportionate to the waiting time.
- Means of communication (phone calls, emails).
- Hotel accommodation if an overnight stay is required.
- Transport between the airport and hotel.
- **Keep all receipts** for reimbursement.

### Missed Connections

- If your flights were booked together (single booking) and you arrive at your final destination **3 or more hours late**, compensation may apply.

## How to Claim

1. Submit a claim via the **Atlas Airways website**.
2. Include your flight details and boarding pass.
3. If your claim is denied, escalate to the relevant **national enforcement body** in the country of departure.

## 24-Hour Money-Back Guarantee

- Cancel any booking for free within **24 hours** of purchase, regardless of fare type.
- Full refund to original payment method.
