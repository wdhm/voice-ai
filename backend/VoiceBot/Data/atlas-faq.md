# Atlas Airways Frequently Asked Questions

## How do I check in online?

Online check-in is available **30 hours to 1 hour before departure**. You can check in via the [Atlas Airways website](https://www.flysas.com) or the Atlas Airways app. After checking in, you will receive a digital boarding pass on your phone or you can print one at home. If you only have carry-on baggage and a digital boarding pass, you can proceed directly to security.

## What baggage is included in my ticket?

Baggage allowance depends on your fare type:

- **Go Light (Europe)**: Personal item only (no carry-on, no checked bag).
- **Go Light (US/Canada/Asia)**: One carry-on + personal item. No checked bag.
- **Go Smart / Go Pro**: One carry-on + personal item + 1 checked bag (23 kg).
- **Atlas Plus Smart / Plus Pro**: One carry-on + personal item + 2 checked bags (23 kg each).
- **Business**: Two carry-on bags + personal item + 2 checked bags (32 kg each).

## Can I change my booking?

Yes. Change fees depend on your fare type:

- **Go**: Fees apply (~€70–€80 for Europe, ~$300–$450 for intercontinental).
- **Plus Pro / Business Pro**: Free rebooking — only pay any fare difference.
- All changes must be made before the scheduled departure time.
- There is a **24-hour money-back guarantee** allowing free cancellation within 24 hours of booking.

## How do I add extra baggage?

Add extra baggage via **"My Trips"** on the Atlas Airways website or app. Booking online in advance is always cheaper than purchasing at the airport. You can also add extra baggage by contacting Atlas Airways Customer Service.

## What if my flight is canceled?

If Atlas Airways cancels your flight, you are entitled to:

- **Free rebooking** to an alternative flight, or
- A **full ticket refund**.
- **EU261 compensation** may apply if the cancellation was within 14 days of departure and not due to extraordinary circumstances.

## What is SkyPoints?

SkyPoints is the Atlas Airways loyalty program with four tiers: **Member, Silver, Gold, and Diamond**. Earn points on flights, hotel stays, car rentals, and credit card purchases. Redeem points for award flights, upgrades, and more. Higher tiers unlock benefits like lounge access, extra baggage, Fast Track, and priority boarding.

## Can I bring my pet?

Yes. Pets can travel in the cabin or as checked baggage, depending on the pet's size, route, and destination. Book pet travel in advance as availability is limited per flight. Service dogs are welcome in the cabin — notify Atlas Airways at least 48 hours before departure.

## Is there Wi-Fi on Atlas Airways flights?

Yes. Wi-Fi is available on most Atlas Airways aircraft. Atlas Airways is rolling out **free Starlink Wi-Fi** across the fleet. Where not yet free, pricing starts from approximately €4.90. SkyPoints Gold and Diamond members receive free Wi-Fi access.

## How early should I arrive at the airport?

- **European/Scandinavian flights**: At least **2 hours** before departure.
- **Intercontinental flights**: At least **3 hours** before departure.

## Can I select my seat in advance?

Yes. Seat selection is available when booking or via "My Trips." It is **free for SkyPoints Gold and Diamond** members. For other fare types, a fee may apply depending on the seat location.

## What happens if my bag is delayed?

Report the missing bag at the **airport baggage service desk** immediately upon arrival. Atlas Airways will track your bag and arrange delivery to your address. You can also follow up via the Atlas Airways website or app. Keep receipts for essential purchases — Atlas Airways may reimburse reasonable expenses.

## Do I need a visa?

Visa requirements depend on your destination and nationality. Check with the **embassy or consulate** of your destination country. Atlas Airways is not responsible for passengers who travel without the required documents.

## Can I bring food on board?

Yes. You may bring food for personal consumption on board. However, **liquids rules apply**: containers must be max 100 ml each, all fitting in a single 1-liter transparent resealable bag. Some destinations may have restrictions on importing food products.

## What is the 24-hour cancellation policy?

You can cancel any Atlas Airways booking for free within **24 hours of purchase**, regardless of fare type. A full refund will be issued to the original payment method. This applies to all bookings made directly with Atlas Airways.
