# Atlas Airways Travel Extras

## Seat Selection

- Pre-select your preferred seat (window, aisle, extra legroom, exit row).
- **Free** for SkyPoints Gold and Diamond members.
- May be chargeable for other ticket types, depending on fare and seat location.

## Meals and Drinks

### Economy (European Flights)

- Complimentary: Coffee, tea, and water.
- Meals and snacks available for purchase on board.
- Pre-order meals available on flights over 80 minutes.

### Economy (Long-Haul Flights)

- Complimentary meals and drinks included.

### Atlas Plus (Premium Economy)

- Enhanced complimentary meals and snacks.
- Complimentary alcoholic beverages.

### Business Class

- Premium multi-course dining with full bar service.
- Pre-select your meal before departure.

### Special Dietary Meals

- Special dietary requirements (vegetarian, vegan, kosher, halal, gluten-free, etc.) can be requested in advance.

## Wi-Fi

- Available on most Atlas Airways flights.
- Atlas Airways is rolling out **free Starlink Wi-Fi** across the fleet.
- Where not yet free, pricing starts from approximately **€4.90**.
- **SkyPoints Gold and Diamond** members get free Wi-Fi access.

## Lounge Access

- **Atlas Plus and Business** passengers receive complimentary Atlas Airways lounge access at key airports.
- Key lounge locations: Copenhagen (CPH), Oslo (OSL), Stockholm Arlanda (ARN), Gothenburg (GOT), Chicago O'Hare (ORD), Newark (EWR).
- **SkyPoints Gold and Diamond** members receive Global Alliance lounge access worldwide.

## Upgrades

- **SkyPoints**: Use points to upgrade your cabin class.
- **Bid for upgrades**: Set your price for a cabin upgrade — Atlas Airways reviews bids before departure.
- Upgrade benefits include: priority check-in, extra baggage allowance, Fast Track security, lounge access, and enhanced meals.
